Our Mission. What we're all about.


Our shop and Wearhouse can be found right next door to eachother.

Everything we do here is to help tackle two issues:

  1. Food Waste
  2. Food Inequality

We do this through working with local businesses and supermarkets. They donate us perfectly good food that would otherwise be thrown away. We then turn this into fresh meals that we provide to our local community through our restaurant and food collection boxes available from our warehouse.
